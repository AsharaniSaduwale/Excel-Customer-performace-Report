## Sales Report :


- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/AsharaniSaduwale/Excel-Customer-performace-Report/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_ 

    **2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and sales targets](https://github.com/AsharaniSaduwale/Excel-Customer-performace-Report/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target%20Report.pdf)_

**Purpose of Sales Analytics:** To monitor and assess business sales performance.

**Importance of Data Analysis**: To detect sales patterns and stay on top of critical KPIs.

**Role of Reports**: To optimize customer discounting, improve consumer negotiations, and uncover international expansion opportunities.ntries.


## Finance Report :

- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/AsharaniSaduwale/Excel-Customer-performace-Report/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)_ & _[Months](https://github.com/AsharaniSaduwale/Excel-Customer-performace-Report/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)_

**Purpose of Financial Analytics:** Evaluates overall financial health, guides strategic decision-making, and streamlines communication with stakeholders.

**Importance of Financial Data Analysis:** Serves as a foundation for budgeting and forecasting while enabling benchmarking against industry peers and historical periods.

**Role of Reports:** Aligns financial planning with long-term strategic goals and instills confidence in the organization’s financial future.
